About Me

Hi, my name is Martin, I’m a book lover and a software engineer. Originally from Argentina, and currently living in Tel Aviv, Israel.

I heard recommendations about a certain book, but couldn’t find it in stores. I asked my friends, but they did not have it, nonetheless I still believed my desired book might be sitting in someone's shelf just waiting for me :)

I started to think if there is a place that I could search for a book and give for exchange one of my own. I love to read books but I have some books I don't read anymore and I would love other people to enjoy it for free.

This website is in Beta stage - if you encounter with any errors or have ideas for improvement, please don’t hesitate to reach out.
